# Agentic Readiness Checklist (Commercial)
This rubric defines the "Definition of Done" for an AI agent before it is exposed to enterprise customers.

### 1. Performance Reliability
* **Latency (TTFT):** Is the Time-to-First-Token under 500ms?
* **Task Success Rate:** Does the agent resolve the goal in >95% of test simulations?

### 2. Trust & Safety
* **Boundary Adherence:** Does the agent strictly follow negative constraints (what NOT to do)?
* **Hallucination Cap:** Are semantic guardrails active to prevent non-factual brand claims?

### 3. Commercial Viability
* **Inference Margin:** Is the cost per resolution lower than the target acquisition cost?
* **Signal Quality:** Does the agent have access to the first-party data needed for high-confidence execution?
