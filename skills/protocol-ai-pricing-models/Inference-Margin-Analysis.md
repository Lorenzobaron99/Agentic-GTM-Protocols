Inference-Margin Analysis & Pricing Framework
In the Agentic era, COGS (Cost of Goods Sold) are dynamic. This model helps PMMs calculate the "Cost of Intelligence" to ensure sustainable gross margins.

1. The Cost of Intelligence (CoI) Calculator
To maintain a healthy B2B margin (typically >80%), the pricing must account for the "Inference Overhead" of autonomous tasks.

Variable	Definition	Example Value
Task Complexity	Avg. tokens required per successful resolution	2,000 tokens
Model Tier	Cost per 1k tokens (e.g., Flash vs. Pro)	$0.01
Inference Cost	(Tokens / 1000) * Model Cost	$0.02 / task
Human Parity Value	Cost for a human to perform the same task	$5.00 / task
2. From "Per-Seat" to "Per-Resolution"
Traditional seat-based pricing fails when an agent replaces the user. We shift to Outcome-Based Pricing:

The Efficiency Peg: Pricing the agent at 20-30% of the cost of a human equivalent.
The Credit System: Users purchase "Resolution Credits," aligning revenue directly with the autonomous work performed.
3. Margin Optimization Strategies
Model Tiering: Route simple intents to "Flash" models and complex reasoning to "Heavy" models to preserve margins.
Cache Efficiency: Using prompt caching to reduce token costs on repetitive enterprise workflows.
